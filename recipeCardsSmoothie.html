Blueberry Smoothie

  • 1 Cup (220g) Blueberry (Frozen or fresh)
  • 1 Cup (220g) Greek Yogurt
  • Honey as you like it
  • 165 ml 2% reduced fat milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/8 tsp ground nutmeg
